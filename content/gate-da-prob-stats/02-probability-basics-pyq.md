## Q5001

[MCQ]
[PYQ: GATE DA 2024]

A fair coin is tossed $n$ times. Let $T$ be the event that the coin shows tails on the first toss, and $S$ be the event that the number of heads equals the number of tails. Which of the following is correct?

- **(A)** $P(T \cap S) = P(T) \cdot P(S)$ is NOT always true
- **(B)** $P(T \cap S) = 0$
- **(C)** $T$ and $S$ are always independent
- **(D)** $P(T \cap S) = P(S)$

**Answer:** A

## Q5002

[MCQ]
[PYQ: GATE DA 2026]

A subset of size 2 is chosen uniformly at random from $\{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}$. What is the probability that the product of the two chosen numbers is even?

- **(A)** $\frac{3}{5}$
- **(B)** $\frac{3}{10}$
- **(C)** $\frac{7}{10}$
- **(D)** $\frac{1}{2}$

**Answer:** C
